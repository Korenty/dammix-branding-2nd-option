INTELLIGENT GEOMETRY

The new mark is not abstract art. It is an engineering diagram of how DAMmix functions within the concrete matrix.

The Structural Matrix

The dark grey cubes represent the aggregate and cement—the raw body of construction. They are arranged in a strong, interlocking isometric formation.

The Active Catalyst

The central orange line and hexagonal drop represent the DAMmix admixture. It penetrates the center of the structure, binding the matrix together.

The Protected Core

The surrounding circle acts as the containment field—guaranteeing consistent performance and protection against environmental stress.
